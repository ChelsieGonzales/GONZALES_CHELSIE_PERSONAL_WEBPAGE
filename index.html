WIZARDING PROFILE

Welcome to my chamber of secrets, where magic meets logic and spells turn into code.

My name is Chelsie Gonzales, but people call me Chechii. I am a proud Muggle-born who somehow got sorted into the magical world of Technology. In my chamber of secrets, creativity meets code, and every project begins with a spark of magic.

CHELSIE'S SECRET CHAMBER

You’ve just opened the Chamber of Secrets but instead of a basilisk, you’ll find me! I’m Chelsie Gonzales, born under the misty skies of Lipa City, Batangas on January 23, 2005. A curious muggle (or maybe not?) who loves to cook, clean, sleep, eat, read, and yap. One thing about me is that I’m endlessly curious about everything, especially how things work, which is the main reason why I chose technology as my path. I’m a total clean-freak (not the OCD kind!), I just love seeing things neat and organized, my clothes are even arranged by color in my closet (hihi). I also adore the feeling of freshly laundered bedsheets and pillowcases, there’s something about it that instantly calms my mind. And lastly, when I was a kid, I had a healthy obsession with Harry Potter. I even created an imaginary friend named Wingardium Leviosa.
